Miyata Ichirou - Techniques

Counter Jab

Miyata’s Counter Jab is a quick, precise jab used to intercept and counter an opponent’s attack. His timing and speed make this technique extremely effective in neutralizing incoming punches.

Jolt Counter

The Jolt Counter is one of Miyata’s most devastating techniques. It involves a sudden, precise counterpunch delivered with such speed and force that it can shock the opponent, effectively turning their attack against them.
